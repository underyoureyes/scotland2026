OPTION B: ScotRail Fort William → Mallaig
Same route, same Glenfinnan Viaduct — diesel not steam. ~10:55 dep → Mallaig 12:45. Return 14:05 → FW 15:55. ~£15–20pp return. Dogs £1–3 each. No advance booking needed — buy on the day.
